# Poto Short Video Animation Plan

## Goal

Create a short 8 to 12 second brand animation for Potoos using Poto, the memory guardian.

This video is for:

- social preview
- landing page hero
- onboarding splash
- future app store preview inspiration
- reference for sprite sheet states

## Is ChatGPT Good For Short Video Animation?

ChatGPT is very good for:

- concept planning
- storyboard writing
- visual prompts
- shot descriptions
- character consistency instructions
- generating still mascot references

For actual video generation, use ChatGPT/Sora only if video generation is available in your account. OpenAI's Sora help docs say the Sora editor can create videos from text and can accept image or video input, with settings for aspect ratio, resolution, duration, variations, and Storyboard timing. That is useful for turning the final Poto reference image into a short concept clip.

Important: AI video is best for **motion exploration**, not final brand control. It can change Poto's face, feather markings, or icon details between frames.

But for production control, do not rely on AI video alone. Use CapCut after generation.

Recommended workflow:

1. Generate final Poto reference image.
2. Generate 4 to 6 key still frames.
3. Use Sora or another image-to-video tool for motion tests.
4. Bring clips into CapCut.
5. Edit timing, music, captions, logo, and export.

Honest recommendation:

- Best for mascot reference: ChatGPT Images.
- Best for quick concept motion: Sora if available, or Runway if you want stronger reference-driven character consistency tests.
- Best for final short promo assembly: CapCut.
- Best for in-app reusable animation later: Rive, Lottie, or a cleaned sprite sheet.

## Best Tool Roles

| Tool | Best Role |
| --- | --- |
| ChatGPT | prompts, storyboard, shot list, copy, reference consistency |
| ChatGPT Images | mascot reference, expression sheet, keyframes |
| Sora | short concept motion and image-to-video tests when available |
| Runway | alternate image/video tool for character consistency tests |
| CapCut | final editing, pacing, music, captions, transitions, export |
| Rive | future in-app vector-style animation |
| Aseprite / TexturePacker | future sprite sheet cleanup/export |

## First Video Concept

Length: 8 to 12 seconds

Format:

- 9:16 vertical for TikTok/Reels/Shorts
- 1:1 square for app/brand preview
- 16:9 optional later

Style:

- premium cute mascot
- deep maroon background
- soft gold particles
- warm cream text
- slow magical motion
- not loud, not hyper

## Storyboard

### Scene 1: Poto Wakes

Time: 0.0s to 2.0s

Visual:

Poto is inside a soft gold camera frame. Eyes closed. A small gold star passes across the screen. Poto opens big golden eyes.

Motion:

Slow blink, tiny head lift, soft gold glow.

Caption:

```text
Meet Poto.
```

### Scene 2: Memories Arrive

Time: 2.0s to 4.5s

Visual:

Small photo and video cards float toward Poto. Poto watches them carefully.

Motion:

Cards drift in, gentle parallax, gold link trail.

Caption:

```text
Your memory guardian.
```

### Scene 3: Original Quality Protected

Time: 4.5s to 7.5s

Visual:

Poto creates a soft gold shield around the cards. The cards stay crisp and glowing.

Motion:

Gold frame closes around memories, subtle sparkle.

Caption:

```text
Original memories, safely shared.
```

### Scene 4: Potoos End Card

Time: 7.5s to 10.0s

Visual:

Poto smiles. Potoos logo/name appears with tagline.

Motion:

Soft zoom out, gold star settles.

Text:

```text
Potoos
Original memories, safely shared.
```

## Sora / Video Prompt

Use this after uploading the final Poto reference image:

```text
Create a premium 10-second vertical brand animation for Potoos, a private photo and video sharing app.

Use the uploaded image as the exact character reference for Poto, the memory guardian mascot. Keep Poto's big golden eyes, soft beige/taupe feathers, dark small beak, rounded cute face, deep maroon and soft gold camera-frame identity.

Scene timing:
0-2s: Poto rests inside a soft gold camera frame on a deep maroon background, eyes closed, then slowly opens glowing golden eyes.
2-4.5s: small photo and video cards float gently toward Poto with subtle gold link trails.
4.5-7.5s: Poto protects the cards with a warm gold shield glow, representing original quality and private sharing.
7.5-10s: Poto smiles softly as the Potoos brand end card appears with the feeling of "Original memories, safely shared."

Mood: warm, premium, gentle, magical, trustworthy, not childish.
Visual style: polished mascot animation, deep maroon velvet background, soft gold particles, warm cream highlights, cinematic lighting, smooth slow motion.

No social media likes, no public feed, no followers, no clutter. Keep the animation simple and brand-focused.
```

## Production Notes

- Generate without text if the video tool creates messy text.
- Add final text inside CapCut instead.
- Keep animation slow and premium.
- Avoid too many cuts.
- Make Poto's eyes the emotional focus.
